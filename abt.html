FeaturesSome of exicting things..

Automatic Stopper

To automatically stop the water flow to ground tank when it is filled and also to display the time required by the tank to be filled based on current flow rate

Drinking Water

To indicate the user whether water for drinking purpose can be filled or not

Bill Payment

To view the bill amount that has to be paid by you for the current usage so that allows you to plan accordingly

Limiting the Usage

Allows you to set the limit of water that has to be used in the home and produces alert when 80% of limit is reached.


Short desc about proj

In this modern world everything is getting automated ,this project is to develop a automated ground tank water controller .By using this device we can overcome the involment of human effort to on/off the water supply for ground water storage tank for which water is provided by the municipality.In the present practice of monitoring the ground tank water controller consists of alarm which raises whe water is filled and we have to stop the flow of water to the tank, this method produces an alternative way of replacing the human involvement to stop water.Internet Of Things(IOT) is controlling of things through internet i.e any device linked with internet through wifi can be controled and monitored through your phone with internet connectivity from anywhere in this world.To increase the comfort of the consumer this device can be controlled through the mobile phone
